Private Pitching Lessons for East Bay Athletes

Vanessa Strong Softball offers private softball pitching lessons in Brentwood, California, serving athletes from Brentwood, Oakley, Antioch, Discovery Bay, and the greater East Bay.

Sessions are built around the athlete in front of us, with instruction that meets beginners, developing pitchers, travel ball players, and high school athletes where they are.

What Lessons Cover

Each lesson focuses on building repeatable mechanics, stronger command, and a more confident approach in the circle.

  • Pitching mechanics and movement quality
  • Spin efficiency, velocity progression, and control
  • Game strategy, hitter reads, and competitive mindset
  • Development plans for youth, middle school, high school, and travel ball pitchers
