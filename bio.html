Meet Vanessa Strong

A standout pitcher and Freedom High School alum, brings years of elite experience after competing at the Division I level for the University of the Pacific. A consistent leader in the circle, she logged over 400 collegiate innings, racked up hundreds of strikeouts, and earned multiple WCC All-Academic honors including multiple Player of the Week honors and team MVP. While posting dominant performances, including complete games, she also earned a Bachelors and two Masters degrees in Kinesology and Education. In high school, she was a four-time First Team All-BVAL selection, two-time league MVP, NCS champion, and Bay Area News Group First Team honoree, earned multiple Cal-Hi All State honors boasting a career 1.48 ERA, 694 strikeouts, and two perfect games.

I help athletes build confidence and command in the circle through detailed mechanics, strategic thinking, and competitive mindset training.

Coaching Philosophy

Every athlete learns differently. Sessions are customized to improve consistency, spin efficiency, control, and game-time decision making.

Who I Train

Beginners, intermediate, and advanced pitchers are all welcome. We build strong fundamentals first, then layer advanced tools and strategy.

Focus Areas

  • Pitch mechanics and movement quality
  • Velocity progression and command
  • Mental approach and confidence
  • Game planning and hitter reads

Service Area

East Bay / Brentwood area, with scheduling options for private instruction and recurring development plans.
