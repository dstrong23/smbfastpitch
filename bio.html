Meet Vanessa Strong

Vanessa Strong is a softball pitching coach serving Brentwood, Oakley, Antioch, Discovery Bay, and the greater East Bay. A former Division I pitcher at the University of the Pacific, Vanessa helps youth, travel ball, and high school pitchers build mechanics, command, confidence, and game awareness.

A standout pitcher and Freedom High School alum, Vanessa brings years of elite experience after competing at the Division I level for the University of the Pacific. A consistent leader in the circle, she logged over 400 collegiate innings, racked up hundreds of strikeouts, and earned multiple WCC All-Academic honors including multiple Player of the Week honors and team MVP.

While posting dominant performances, including complete games, she also earned a Bachelors and two Masters degrees in Kinesiology and Education.

In high school, she was a four-time First Team All-BVAL selection, two-time league MVP, NCS champion, and Bay Area News Group First Team honoree. She also earned multiple Cal-Hi All State honors while posting a career 1.48 ERA, 694 strikeouts, and two perfect games.

"I help athletes build confidence and command in the circle through detailed mechanics, strategic thinking, and competitive mindset training."

Division I Pitching Experience

Vanessa provides private softball pitching instruction for athletes in Brentwood and nearby East Bay communities, combining collegiate-level experience with practical, athlete-specific coaching.

Coaching Philosophy

Every athlete learns differently. Sessions are customized to improve consistency, spin efficiency, control, and game-time decision making.

Pitching Lessons for All Skill Levels

Beginners, intermediate, and advanced pitchers are all welcome. We build strong fundamentals first, then layer advanced tools and strategy.

Softball Development Focus Areas

  • Pitch mechanics and movement quality
  • Velocity progression and command
  • Mental approach and confidence
  • Game planning and hitter reads
